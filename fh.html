OceanWP is an extremely versatile theme with excellent support systems in place.

Any user, beginner to pro, will have the tools to build and plenty of ways to get help as needed. I am a relative beginner at WordPress site-building and wasn’t looking for a ‘free’ option (though OceanWP has one). I wanted all the bells and whistles of a versatile theme along with reliable support so I can focus on learning while making my new endeavors in the www space successful.

In my opinion, this versatile theme + support is the best.
